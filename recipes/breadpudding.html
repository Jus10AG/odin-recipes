Bread Pudding

Description

Every home cook needs a go-to bread pudding recipe. This traditional version is

decadent, delicious, and easy to make with basic ingredients. It will quickly find

a permanent home in your recipe box.

Prep Time:

15 mins

Cook Time:

45 mins

Total Time:

1 hr

Servings:

~ 12

Yield:

1 (8x8-inch) pan

Ingredients:

  • 6 slices day-old bread, torn into small pieces
  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter, melted
  • ½ cup raisins (Optional)
  • 2 cups milk
  • ¾ cup white sugar
  • 4 large eggs, beaten
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

What to Do:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C)

  2. Place bread pieces into an 8-inch square baking pan.

    Drizzle melted butter over bread and sprinkle raisins over top

  3. Whisk milk, sugar, eggs, cinnamon, and vanilla together in

    4. a medium mixing bowl until well combined. Pour mixture over

    bread, and lightly push down with a fork until all bread is

    covered and soaking up the liquid

  4. Bake in the preheated oven until golden brown and the top

    springs back when lightly pressed, about 45 minutes.
